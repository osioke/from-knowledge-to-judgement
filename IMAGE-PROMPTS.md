# AI Image Generation Prompts
## From Knowledge to Judgement — White Paper Figures

Use these prompts in any AI image generator (Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Adobe Firefly,
Ideogram, or similar). Each prompt is written to produce a clean, professional
diagram or illustration suitable for a white paper.

For best results: generate at 1200×675px, 16:9 aspect ratio, PNG format.

---

## hero-banner.png
**Used on:** Home page / Executive Summary  
**Purpose:** Opening visual — sets the tone of the paper

**Prompt:**
```
A clean, modern editorial illustration of a single professional figure standing
at the intersection of five glowing pathways, each pathway labelled with a different
discipline. The figure faces forward with confident posture. The background is dark
navy or deep charcoal. The pathways are rendered as soft light beams in different
colours: blue, amber, green, red, and white. The overall style is flat design with
subtle depth — not photorealistic, not cartoonish. No text. No faces visible.
Aspect ratio 16:9. Minimalist. Professional.
```

## General Style Notes for Consistency

Apply these to all images for a cohesive look:

- **Background:** White (#FFFFFF) or very light grey (#F8F8F8)
- **Primary accent:** Deep orange (#E65100) or amber (#FF8F00)
- **Secondary colours:** Deep navy (#1A237E), charcoal (#263238), dark green (#1B5E20)
- **Typography (if any):** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Helvetica, or similar)
- **Style:** Flat design with minimal shadows — avoid gradients, textures, or 3D effects
- **No people:** Avoid human figures unless the image specifically calls for one (hero-banner only)
- **Dimensions:** 1200×675px, 16:9, PNG
